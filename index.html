Welcome! My name is Ariel Tello Fallau, I am a PhD student at the Georgia Institute of Technology, specializing in space plasma physics with a focus on planetary science. My current research explores the interaction between Jupiter’s magnetosphere and the subsurface ocean of its moon, Europa. A key aspect of my work involves studying Europa’s induced magnetic field, which plays a crucial role in identifying the properties of this hidden ocean, such as its depth and salinity. Through this research, I aim to advance our understanding of Europa’s potential habitability.
